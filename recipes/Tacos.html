Tacos

A popular Mexican dish consisting of a folded or rolled tortilla filled with various ingredients such as meat, beans, cheese, lettuce, and salsa. Tacos are often customizable, with a wide variety of regional flavors.

Ingredients:

  • 8 small corn or flour tortillas
  • 1 lb ground beef or chicken breast
  • 1 packet taco seasoning (or a mix of chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, paprika)
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or Monterey Jack)
  • ½ cup sour cream
  • ½ cup salsa
  • Olive oil for cooking

Steps:

  1. Prepare the Meat: Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add ground beef or chicken and cook until browned. Drain excess fat if needed.
  2. Season the Meat: Add taco seasoning and a bit of water to the meat. Simmer for 5 minutes until the seasoning is fully absorbed.
  3. Prepare the Tortillas: Warm the tortillas in a dry pan or wrap them in foil and place in the oven for a few minutes.
  4. Assemble the Tacos: In each tortilla, place a spoonful of seasoned meat. Top with shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, and shredded cheese.
  5. Add Condiments: Add sour cream and salsa as desired.
  6. Serve: Serve the tacos warm with lime wedges on the side.
